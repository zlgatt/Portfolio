Redefining brilliance, and creativity.

You don’t need to reinvent the wheel to be brilliant. And you can’t buy ‘brilliant’.
Brilliance is resilience, dedication, and a passion to move forward.

I know brilliance. It’s the cups of instant coffee in the wee hours of the morning. It’s the scribbles and crumpled paper on a crumby desk. It’s the itchiness and the fervour of 19 tabs open at once. It’s the ecstasy of breakthrough at the point of the most intimate frustration.
