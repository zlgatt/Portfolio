Defining a Zoë;

An over-zealous design student, photographer, and content creator. She's medically certified to be intolerant to all things norm, and bases her diet on going against the flow. ️

With a voice that can be heard from miles away, this unstoppable extrovert will strategize a brand concept, interactive marketing, and experiential design displays before you could finish the word “idea”. Trust me, she’s been tested.️

Born on August 5th '98, creative skills were noticable to close relatives from an early age. Her 8 year dream of becoming a fashion designer was over by the age of 14, when she realised that her portfolio was too concise. After investing in a black plastic A3 book-folder, she began her compilation.

Roughly 6 years later, the soon-to-be graduate holds a vast portfolio ranging from editorial design, photography, illustration, social media content, installations, brand concepts, packaging designs, augmented reality animations, virtual reality videography, and more, proudly.
